== PRIVACY POLICY ==

1. Data We Collect
- Account: your email address (for login) and a system-generated user ID.
- Profile: a random nickname.
- Posts: photos you upload, required whisky name, optional description, EXIF location data and photo timestamp.
- Technical: timestamps, usage events, error logs.

2. How We Use It
- To provide account login and post publishing.
- To display your posts on the map and enable discovery.
- To maintain reliability and trustworthiness of whisky information.
- To ensure safety and prevent abuse.
- We never sell or share your personal data.
- We may analyze, aggregate, and share anonymized usage patterns (e.g. popular whiskies, regions) with partners. These insights never identify individual users.

3. Who Can See Your Data
- Other users: only your nickname and posts.
- Admins: may access your account info only for support or legal compliance.

4. Storage & Retention
- Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud, region: asia-northeast1, Tokyo).
- Authentication data (email, UID) is stored securely on Google servers.
- Data is kept until you delete your account.
- Inactive accounts may be removed after a period of inactivity.
- Upon account deletion:
  - Your email and authentication details are erased.
  - Your posts remain as anonymized content, no longer linked to you.

5. Your Rights
- You may access, correct, and delete your data at any time in Settings → Privacy.
- We do not provide automated data export.
