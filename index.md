# WhiskyPin

**WhiskyPin** is a community-driven mobile app that helps whisky enthusiasts discover where interesting whiskies can be enjoyed around the world. You can:

- Share where you’ve tasted unique drams  
- Explore hidden spots to try whiskies by the glass  
- Unlock community posts using credits  

---

## Key Features
- 📍 **Map-based discovery** – See where whiskies have been spotted  
- 🥃 **Community posts** – Photos, notes, and verified locations  
- 🔑 **Unlock location** – Contribute to explore
